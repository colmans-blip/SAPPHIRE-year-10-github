Main Tikanga of marae

All marae have different protocols and rules. To know these rules and make sure you don't enter the marae in a bad state, here are the main tikanga that you should know before even thinking of stepping on a marae.

  • When you enter the marae, ALWAYS remove your shoes. Asoloutely no kai in the marae unless you have been given permission to eat.
  • Do not walk onto the marae without a greeting or consent from the tangata whenua
  • Always respect the kaumatua, for they hold the most wisdom and matauranga
  • Prevent yourself from sitting on tables, or any places that isn't on a chair or the floor
